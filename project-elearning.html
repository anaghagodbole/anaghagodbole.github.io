ABOUT THE PROJECT

Background: I worked as a Frontend Engineer for the American Heart Association's E-learning platform, which aimed to offer an interactive learning experience for users focused on heart health education. The platform allowed users to enroll in various subscription-based courses, including paid premium and free courses, and earn completion certificates. It also featured collaborative tools for users to engage with content like courses, presentations, and tests.

Timeline: 8 months

My Role: As a Frontend Engineer, I developed the End-User Portal and Admin Portal. The End-User Portal enabled interaction with eLearning content, while the Admin Portal allowed content providers to upload course material and manage analytics, including data visualizations such as tables, graphs, and charts.

Tools used: Angular 11, HTML, Bootstrap 4, CSS/SCSS, Custom-libraries, JIRA, Bitbucket, Jenkins
