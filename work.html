E-Learning Platform

Web Design, UI/UX

Project Overview

This comprehensive e-learning platform was designed to provide an intuitive and engaging learning experience for students across various disciplines. The project involved creating a responsive interface that works seamlessly across devices while maintaining a focus on content accessibility and user engagement.

The design incorporates modern UI principles with a clean, distraction-free layout that helps students focus on their learning materials. Special attention was given to the course navigation system, video player interface, and interactive quiz components to enhance the learning experience.
